Format and add a story
Two steps. First format your story, then paste it here.
Step one — format your story like this 
Rewrite the source as a single story in EXACTLY this template. Output only this and nothing else.

CATEGORY: A True Story then a middot then one theme word such as Courage or Survival
TITLE: a compelling title
DETAIL: place, year then a middot then about N minutes
STORY:
Tell it as flowing narrative using only real, verifiable facts. Spell out all numbers. No headings, no bullet points. Separate each paragraph with a blank line.
SUMMARY:
Three or four sentences recapping what actually happened.
LESSON:
One or two sentences: the deeper takeaway a reader could carry into their own life.
